# Store Listing — PA MHAD App

## Google Play Store

### App Name
PA Mental Health Advance Directive

### Short Description (80 chars max)
Create your Pennsylvania Mental Health Advance Directive with guided forms.

### Full Description

PA Mental Health Advance Directive (MHAD) helps Pennsylvania residents create and manage Mental Health Advance Directives under PA Act 194 of 2004. This app is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. This app does not provide medical, legal, or therapeutic advice. Consult an attorney or healthcare provider for advice specific to your situation.

Create your directive using a guided step-by-step wizard. Choose from three form types: Combined Directive (declaration + power of attorney), Declaration Only, or Power of Attorney Only. The app includes:

- Step-by-step guided form completion
- Educational content about PA MHADs
- AI assistant for general questions (powered by Google Gemini)
- PDF generation for printing and signing
- Crisis resources (988 Lifeline, Crisis Text Line, SAMHSA)
- Privacy modes (public/private with biometric lock)

Your directive documents your mental health treatment preferences in advance, in case you are ever unable to make decisions for yourself. All data stays on your device unless you choose to use the optional AI assistant.

This app is intended for adults (18+) in Pennsylvania. Directives must be printed, signed, and witnessed to be legally valid under PA Act 194.

## Google Play Data Safety Section

### Data collected

**Health info** (mental health treatment preferences)
- Collected: Yes
- Shared: No
- Purpose: App functionality
- Storage: Local device only
- User can request deletion: Yes (via "Delete All Data" in app menu)

**User content** (text sent to AI assistant)
- Collected: Yes (only when AI assistant is used)
- Shared: Yes, with Google Gemini API (only when AI features are actively used)
- Purpose: App functionality (answering user questions)
- Storage: Not stored persistently by the app; Google's retention policies apply on their end
- User can request deletion: Users can avoid data sharing by not using the AI feature

### Data NOT collected
- Personal identifiers (not transmitted off device)
- Financial info
- Location
- Contacts
- Browsing history
- Device identifiers
- Diagnostics or crash logs (none collected)

### Security
- Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS for AI API calls)
- Optional biometric authentication for app access
- Data can be deleted via "Delete All Data" in app menu
- No account or login required
